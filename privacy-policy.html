Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

This privacy policy applies to Portfolio (“the app”), a privacy-focused portfolio tracker for stocks, ETFs, mutual funds, precious metals, cryptocurrencies, and bank accounts, available on iOS and macOS.

Data collection

Portfolio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage data to our servers or any third party. The app does not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. We do not have access to your portfolio data, account names, or holdings.

Data stored on your device

All your portfolio data (holdings, accounts, instruments, prices you enter) is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. This data never leaves your device except:

  • Optional iCloud sync: If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synced using your own Apple ID to your own iCloud account. We do not have access to it.
  • Public market data: The app may request public market data (e.g. ticker prices, fund NAVs) from public APIs to refresh prices. No account linking or personal identifiers are sent; only instrument identifiers (e.g. ticker symbols, ISINs) are used to fetch public data.

Children’s privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from users. It is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page and, when relevant, will be released with a new version of the app. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest revision.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app, please contact us through the support channel provided in the App Store listing for Portfolio.
